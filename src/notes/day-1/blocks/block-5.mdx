## Assignment: Create a fully functional HTML (only) website for a small business.

Instructions:

1. Research a small business (e.g. a local bakery, coffee shop, boutique).
2. Create a homepage that includes the business name, logo, and brief introduction.
3. Design a navigation menu with links to pages for the business's products, services, location, and contact information.
4. On the products page, create a grid layout of images and descriptions of the business's offerings.
5. On the services page, provide a list of services that the business offers and descriptions of each.
6. On the location page, include an interactive map showing the business's location
